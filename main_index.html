About

The App

Goals

  • Log results (blood sugars and exercise)
  • Watch the trends over time
  • Set realistic and personal goals
  • Share results with whom you choose (Dr /Diabetes Health Professionals/ Others)

Roadmap

  • Store your results so you can access them anywhere
  • Add a food diary if you want to track food
  • Make it social - connect with other users and ask for help
  • Create a native mobile app

How it works

Follow these basic steps

1. Add a result to the log book - recording the details that are important to you

  • Name: Name it with a relative time, e.g. Waking or Dinner, this helps with looking at your results later.
  • Result: Record the value of your result.
  • When: The time and date will be automatically set to when you added the result but you can change it.
  • Exercise: If you want to track your exercise you can add just these values (they will be excluded from the results graph)
  • Label: would you like to label this result for later reference? e.g. sickday
  • Comments: any other comments? You may just leave this for period where you are adjusting or trying to work out whats going on.

2. Once you have been collecting results for a while you can see these plotted on the results graph.

3. You also filter results in the logbook view - maybe you want to look at only results at breakfast?

  • Filter by relative time e.g. "wak+bre" will show all waking and breakfast results
  • Filter results by value. e.g. ">10" will show all sugars over 10, and likewise "<5" will show all those results
